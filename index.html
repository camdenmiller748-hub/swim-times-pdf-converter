Part 1: Generate the PDF in Team Manager

  1. Open Team Manager on your computer and go to: Reports ‚Üí Performance Reports ‚Üí Top Times
  2. You will see a settings window. Match all settings as shown in this screenshot:
  3. Configure these exact settings:
    • Team: Select your team from the dropdown
    • Gen: All
    • Use Times Since: Enter your start date (like 10/30/25) and CHECK the box next to "Use Since Date"
    • Sort by: Name
    • Prelim / Finals Filter: All
    • Splits: None
    • Course Options: Select "YY (Convert to Y, Show Y)" - the YY option is required
    • Event Filters: Individual (NOT Relay)
    • Top How Many?: 1
    • Layout: 1 Column
  4. Click the "Create Report" button at the bottom
  5. A PDF will be generated. Save the PDF to your computer (remember where you save it!)

Part 2: Upload and Convert the PDF

  1. Scroll down on this page until you see a gray box that says "Click to upload or drag & drop your PDF here"
  2. Click on that gray box and select the PDF you just saved from Team Manager
  3. Wait a few seconds while the PDF is processed. You will see a message saying "Processing PDF..."
  4. When it's done, you'll see "Found X swimmers" in green

Part 3: Copy Data to Google Sheets

  1. Scroll down more until you see a section titled "Data Ready to Copy"
  2. Click the green "Copy Data" button (you should see "Copied!" appear briefly)
  3. Open the Google Sheets document
  4. Ensure you're on school's sheet tab at the bottom of Google Sheets
  5. Click on cell A5 (this is column A, row 5 - the first empty row under the headers)
  6. Press Ctrl+V on your keyboard (or Cmd+V if you're on a Mac)
  7. The data will fill in automatically across all columns. Done!
